---
title: Picture Takin’ Blues
date: 2025-08-30
modDate: 2025-09-16
---

My camera is a little purse
for all the rolls of film I haven’t used

So much to take a picture of
and I don’t know how I could ever choose

I’ve been all around this world
up all the roads, and down the avenues

But I haven’t pressed the shutter yet,
so I just sing the picture takin blues
